Connect your master Google Sheet. One sheet powers everything — registration, check-in, meal tracking, admin.

Your Google Sheet headers (Row 1)

Make sure your sheet has these exact headers:

GuestID | FirstName | LastName | Email | Phone | Tabernacle | AgeGroup | PaymentType | BalanceNotes | CheckinStatus | RegisteredAt | Notes | B_Mar31 | L_Mar31 | B_Apr1 | L_Apr1 | B_Apr2 | L_Apr2 | B_Apr3 | L_Apr3 | B_Apr4 | L_Apr4 | B_Apr5 | L_Apr5 | B_Apr6 | L_Apr6 | B_Apr7 | L_Apr7 | B_Apr8 | L_Apr8

2

Publish as CSV (for reading all members)

In your Google Sheet: File → Share → Publish to web. Select Sheet1 and CSV. Click Publish. Paste the URL below.

3

Deploy Apps Script (for saving new data)

In your Sheet: Extensions → Apps Script. Paste the script at the bottom of this page. Deploy → New Deployment → Web App → Anyone. Paste the URL below.
